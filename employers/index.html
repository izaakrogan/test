Our People

Our students are exceptionally motivated. Applicants need to put in around 200h of work before we consider them in the selection process. The 5% of applicants that get excepted are passionate learners. We value hard work, talent and diversity - with around 40% of our intake being female and many from groups underrepresented in the tech industry. Our selection process is simple: We take people we would want to work with at our own startups.

Our Engineers

By the end of the 16 week programme, our students have clocked up more than 1000 hours of developer time. Our engineers will excel on your team from day one.
